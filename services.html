Andrew's Tutoring Services

Individual or Group

Some students prefer to learn on their own. Some subject work is best learned in groups. Andrew's Tutoring Services is pleased to offer both individual and group tutoring options.

Online or In-Person

Because everyone's learning style is as unique as they are, Andrew's Tutoring Services offers academic assistance both online and in-person.

Subjects

Math

Andrew's Tutoring Services offers assistance in K-12 Mathematics concepts including Algebra, Trigonometry, Calculus, Statistics, and more.

Science

Andrew's Tutoring Services offers assistance in K-12 Scientific concepts including The Scientific Method, Biology, Chemistry, Physics (both Algebra- and Calculus-based), and more.

Computer Science

Andrew's Tutoring Services offers assistance in Computer Science concepts including software design, data structures, algorithms, memory allocation, debugging, and more. In additon, Andrew's Tutoring Services offers assistance in specific programming languages including C, C++, Java, Python, BASH/Shell, MATLAB, HTML/CSS, and more.

Writing and Grammar

Andrew's Tutoring Services offers assistance in Writing and Grammar. Accurate and understandable editing services are available through both synchronous workshops and asynchronous editing and review.

Other subjects available upon request
