My Inspiration

Due to current circumstances, students across the United States have not been able to attend in-person classes. Because of this, many students are not able to receive the academic assistance provided through in-person education. I believe students deserve the one-on-one help they would receive through their normal schooling. Andrew's Tutoring Services are available in a one-on-one format, online or in-person.

My Education

A proud alumnus of Henrico County Public Schools, I attended Echo Lake Elementary School, Hungary Creek Middle School, and Mills E. Godwin High School's Specialty Center for Science, Math, and Technology. I am now pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science. As the high school would suggest, my favorite subjects are math and science.

My Interests

A love of technology can be found in everything I do. From video games to RC cars and planes, I enjoy creating and playing with the world around me. In high school, I was a member of TALON 540, Godwin's FIRST Robotics Competition team. I found my love of engineering on the team and it has led me to many fun projects.
